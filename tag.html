Prestige Institute of Management Gwalior

Accredited “A” grade by NAAC, Prestige Institute of Management is one of the
reputed private institutions in Gwalior, MP. It has been ranked 7th amongst best
BBA and BCA colleges by GHRDC and 8th for Admission process amongst the
B schools in India.

PIM Gwalior offers admission into BBA, BCA, BTM, B.Sc, B.Com and LLB courses on merit basis and interview. With the average package of 3.27 LPA offered, B.Com course has become the most preferred option by students, with an ROI of 111%. Both regular and integrated MBA programs, accredited by NBA are offered at PIM.

Admission for regular MBA is done through CMAT score only. PIM also avails short term certificate programs in management. One can avail those through basic application procedure. Applicants have to follow the counseling procedure for final admissions through DTE, Madhya Pradesh. Based on rank scored, candidates will receive admission letters to their mail ID by the end of each round.

With 54% of the batch strength comprising of female candidates, PIM shows gender diversity in its acceptance trends. And the faculty to student ratio is almost 1:8, making it an effective academic atmosphere.

Candidates interested in Banking and Finance sectors will find multiple job offers during placement season of PIM. With an average salary of 4.52 LPA being offered, PIM brings profitable ROI of 102% for MBA aspiring candidates
